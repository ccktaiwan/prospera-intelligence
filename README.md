Prospera Intelligence Co., Ltd.

Prospera Intelligence is a human-centric intelligence system that enables AI governance, system evolution, and enterprise decision-making at scale.

Prospera Intelligence exists because every generation of technology will change — but the need for human-centered intelligence, governance, and principled system evolution will not.

Authority Boundary

Authority Boundary Statement:
This repository defines purpose, principles, and human-centric intelligence foundations for the Prospera ecosystem. It does not define execution logic, enforcement mechanisms, or system authority. Canonical governance and execution definitions reside exclusively in the prospera-os repository.

Purpose and Scope

Prospera Intelligence defines the why behind the Prospera ecosystem.

It establishes the enduring principles that must remain invariant across technological generations, including:

Human-centered intelligence

Governance-first thinking

Accountable system evolution

Decision-making grounded in responsibility rather than automation

Prospera Intelligence does not implement execution systems, operating constraints, or enforcement logic.
Those responsibilities belong to downstream execution systems.

Relationship to Prospera OS

Prospera OS is the execution-layer operating system derived from the principles defined by Prospera Intelligence.

The relationship is strictly directional:

Prospera Intelligence defines purpose and invariant principles

Prospera OS implements governed execution and enforcement

Prospera Intelligence does not override, duplicate, or redefine system authority established in Prospera OS.

Canonical execution authority resides in:
https://github.com/ccktaiwan/prospera-os

What Prospera Intelligence Is (and Is Not)
Prospera Intelligence IS

A human-centric intelligence foundation

A source of long-term governance principles

A framework for responsible system evolution

A philosophical and strategic anchor for AI-era organizations

Prospera Intelligence IS NOT

An execution operating system

A governance enforcement engine

A workflow or automation platform

A replacement for Prospera OS

Role in the Prospera Ecosystem

Prospera Intelligence provides:

The ethical and conceptual grounding for AI governance

The invariants that constrain system evolution

The principles that guide enterprise-scale decision-making

Execution systems derived from Prospera Intelligence must preserve these invariants, but are responsible for their own enforcement mechanisms.

Design Philosophy

Prospera Intelligence assumes that:

Technology evolves faster than institutions

Intelligence without governance creates risk

Systems must be designed to remain accountable over time

Therefore, Prospera Intelligence prioritizes:

Human judgment over automated authority

Governance before optimization

Long-term system integrity over short-term efficiency
